Refactory

Welcome to our Factory game, created for team week at Epicodus!

These are the controls:

  • Move with the arrow keys
  • Click on a square to mine the square
  • With Miner or Smelter selected, click where you want to place
  • Press the 0 key to cycle between equipped tools or choose from the list above the HUD
